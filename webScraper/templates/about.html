The Food Atlas is an interactive data visualisation map of food businesses in the Perth metropolitan region. The map shows the borders of the various Local Government Areas (LGAs). Hovering the mouse cursor over any LGA will display important statics about it, such as the density and number of fast-food restaurants, grocery stores, and cafes. Heatmaps based on these important statistics can be turned on to provide a broader idea of how food businesses are distributed throughout Perth.
